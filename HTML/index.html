Why is it important to be physically fit?

If you would like to be healthy and lead a lively life, it is very important that you are physically fit. If you are physically fit, then you can make sure that the strength as well as the endurance of your body is significantly improved and in addition to that, it will also improve your lifestyle. It is not easy to maintain a physically fit body most especially if we always feel stressed and you always feel the strain of your modern lifestyle, but if you can exercise regularly and enjoy a number of excellent recreations, then, this will not be so hard after all.

Be active

You need to be spending at least 3 hours a week exercising or being active. There are 336-hour in a week so you should prioritise at least 3 hours a week for exercise. Split in to 30min-60min session this can be very achievable even for extremely busy people. If you want to be in the best shape of your life, then commitment and hard work are essential.

Aerobic exercise

Cardiorespiratory fitness can be measured using VO2 max, a measure of the amount of oxygen the body can uptake and utilize. Aerobic exercise, which improves cardiorespiratory fitness, involves movement that increases the heart rate to improve the body's oxygen consumption. This form of exercise is an important part of all training regiments ranging from professional athletes to the everyday person. Also, it helps increase stamina.
